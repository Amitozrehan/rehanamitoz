GURU NANAK DEV ENGINEERING COLLEGE

Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College (GNDEC), established in 1956 by the Nankana Sahib Education Trust (NSET), is one of the oldest and prestigious engineering institutions in Northern India.

Founded to uplift rural and economically weaker sections, the college admits 70% of students from rural areas. Initially affiliated with Punjab University, it is now under I.K. Gujral Punjab Technical University (IKGPTU).

It has received TEQIP-II and III grants from MHRD and DST-FIST funding for research. The college consistently ranks among the top 50 engineering institutes nationally and has received awards for placement excellence.

It offers six UG engineering branches, BCA, BBA, B.Com, B.Voc, B.Arch, 11 PG programs, and Ph.D. in all engineering fields. GNDEC also boasts strong NCC, NSS, and sports participation, and has produced over 37,000 graduates globally.
